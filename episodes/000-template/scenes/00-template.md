# Template scene

Public scene content goes here. Write the scene the way the DM will read it at the table — short paragraphs, evocative description, dialog beats. Markdown.

Underleaf scenes typically open in medias res: the PCs are already where they need to be, doing what they were doing, when the curtain rises. The DM reads, the players react.

Avoid stage directions that PCs would not know ("the NPC is lying" — instead, "the NPC's eye twitches"). Hidden material — what the NPC is actually thinking, what's behind the door, what's about to happen — lives in `../dm/`.
