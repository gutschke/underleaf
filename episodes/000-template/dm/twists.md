# Template — DM-only notes

DM-only notes for an episode's scenes live here. Typical structure:

- **The hidden state of each scene.** What the NPCs actually know, what they want, what they are concealing. What the room contains that the PCs do not see at first glance.
- **The plausibility-tier table** for any casts the PCs are likely to attempt during this episode. The AI prompt-bar in the Quire runtime can help generate these, but pre-thinking the obvious ones is faster at the table.
- **Stress and harm triggers** specific to this episode.
- **Reveal beats.** What information becomes available to the PCs if they ask the right questions, succeed on a check, or push the scene in a particular direction.
- **Branches.** Where this episode can fork based on player choices, and what each branch leads to.
- **Connection to the main arc.** Which arc beats this episode touches. The PCs should not feel the arc until much later; the DM keeps the map.

For the template episode this file is a placeholder. Replace the body with actual DM notes when you author a real episode.
