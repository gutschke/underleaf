# Non-player characters

This folder holds NPC records as they are added to the campaign. NPCs the players have *met* are safe to read about; NPCs who appear later or who carry secrets may include `dmNotes` or live partly under DM-only material.

If you are a player, treat unfamiliar names with care — looking up an NPC by name to "research" them before the campaign has introduced them is a form of self-spoiler. The Quire runtime handles this for you (NPCs are only surfaced as the DM reveals them), but the raw repo does not enforce it.
